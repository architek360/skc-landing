Where the whole city runs as a startup.

Google Fiber has acted like a lightening rod to attract a lot of startups and venture capitalists to the Kansas City area. It has also energized the local community and entrepreneurs in to action. StartupKC is a group of such startups in Kansas City Startup Village that are pushing the limit of Google Fiber to innovate.
