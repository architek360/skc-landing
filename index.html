StartupKC

Where the whole city runs as a startup

An enterprise requires logistics, legal, human resources, marketing, publicity, finance and technology. A large business usually has separate departments to meet these needs. A startup on the other hand has founders, mentors, an incubator and here in Kansas City Startup Village they also have a wonderful community that has stepped up to fill the void. This effort is being led by volunteers. StartupKC is an initiave to facilitate openness, allow participation and organize various activities under the umbrella of Kansas City Startup Village.

How do we do it?

StartupKC uses Bugzilla as the primary tool. Bugzilla has been used by successful startups to manage their products. We are using Bugzilla to run the city itself as a one big startup.


What is Bugzilla?
A bug is opened on behalf of the customer to record any difference between the observed and expected state of a product. A bug could be any actioanable task or an item which is assigned to a product. Our products are various initiatives led by co-leaders. Our customer is a startup. Bugzilla is designed to manage our product, assign resources to it and monitor the progress all the way to completion.

Why is Bugzilla a good fit?
It was inspired by Matthew and Adam's manifesto.
- It can funnel ideas / problems / projects in a structured and query-able format.
- It can identify the top 10 or the top 100 priorities and also draw dependency charts.
- It allows individuals to give their inputs without excessive policing.
- It allows individuals to vote and has built-in checks against flooding.
